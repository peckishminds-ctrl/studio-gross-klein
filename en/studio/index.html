About

Enthusiasm for quality — in materiality, finishing, or function. A holistic interest in product, people, and environment. That is what studio gross klein stands for.

Expertise

All aspects of product development — from ideation to production. With an outside perspective we challenge the status quo and help companies develop new ideas and concepts. We bring experience in developing wood, metal, and plastic products and deep knowledge of how they behave in use and manufacturing.
