Internship at studio gross klein

You experience how real products grow from the first idea to market readiness — and learn every important step of product development. From toys to consumer goods, our project range stays wide.

At the start of each semester, preferably for six months, we look for design students completing a mandatory internship. We currently cannot offer school internships etc. Skills in common tools are welcome; what matters is enthusiasm for good products, creativity, and the joy of making. Besides a model workshop and photo studio we offer inspiring workspaces with a view of greenery.
