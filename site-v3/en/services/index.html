Our services cover the full process of sound product development — from the first idea to market launch.

  1. Briefing
    Placeholder — service imagery

    Every project starts with a thorough briefing. Together with our clients we answer the key questions: what, how, where, when, with what, and why. Once the relevant information is in place, we produce a specification and give direct feedback. We are happy to prepare a tailored quote including everything the project needs — so we can develop the right products for you.

  2. Ideation
    Placeholder — service imagery

    We are good at finding strong solutions and new ideas. That is not magic — it is applying methods and techniques that have proven successful. We explore a wide range of directions, then shortlist the strongest ideas and research their distinctiveness, always looking for concepts with an authentic story. We refine the outcome and present it exclusively to our client.

  3. Visualization
    Placeholder — service imagery

    Products need to be visualized for many purposes — especially before a full model exists. We produce professional drawings and renderings, i.e. realistic 3D visuals. Vector graphics for instruction manuals, infographics, illustrations from logos to entire children's books are all possible. With our in-house photo studio we also offer professional product shoots.

  4. Model making
    Placeholder — service imagery

    To evaluate a big idea, it helps to test it small. For each phase and requirement we can build the right model: volume, functional, presentation, spatial, 1:1, and more — in paper, card, wood, metal, or plastic, in our model-making workshop. We offer rapid prototyping (3D printing), precise contour cutting across many materials with our laser cutter, and vacuum forming for plastic parts such as housings so we can implement design solutions directly and in detail.

  5. Development
    Placeholder — service imagery

    This is where ideas become reality. We gather all data needed for implementation and clarify key questions: which materials and processes, which volumes and price points, and more. We coordinate the process and prepare technical data — 3D CAD, 2D construction plans, technical drawings. Close communication with manufacturers is essential so production stays transparent and smooth. We can accompany production, because experience shows adjustments are often needed at the start of manufacturing.

  6. Safety
    Placeholder — service imagery

    We come from the toy industry, which sets the highest bar for product safety. With additional training as certified toy safety specialists we know which safety requirements and warnings apply. We also support risk assessments where needed. Does the product need assembly after purchase, or is it self-explanatory to use? We dive into the details and create assembly guides or user instructions.

  7. Testing
    Placeholder — service imagery

    Function. Stability. Handling. Safety. User satisfaction. A product must be tested thoroughly before it goes into production — that is how weaknesses surface early. When needed, we coordinate and run testing. You stay unburdened: technical responsibility stays in one place and communication stays short and efficient.

  8. Network
    Placeholder — service imagery

    Graphic designers, photographers, web developers, copywriters, strategists, programmers. Through our expert network we also take on projects beyond core product development. When needed we assemble a flexible team that bundles skills and works efficiently — while everyone speaks the same language: yours.

Everything starts with your project. Get in touch and tell us about it.
